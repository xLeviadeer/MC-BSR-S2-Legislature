**Trash Cans** are a small [[Government|governmentally]] regulated structure used for the disposal of items. **Trash Cans** are exempt from [[Jurisdiction#Hard Jurisdiction|Hard Jurisdiction]] on the [[Jurisdiction#Containment of Hazardous Materials|Containment of Hazardous Materials]] as long as they meet regulation.

## Trash Can Regulations
1. **Trash Cans** must *exactly* match the construction of one of multiple possible predetermined **Trash Can** construction options as determined by the [[Government]]. **Trash Cans** may also be validated on a case-by-case basis via an [[Elected Officials|Elected Official's]] approval.
2. **Trash Cans** do *not* require a [[Jurisdiction#Containment of Hazardous Materials|hazardous materials signage]].
3. **Trash Cans** must be provided as part of a structure or within a reasonable distance of a zone of structures within [[Land#Hard Land|Hard Land]].
4. The [[Government]] does *not* provide resources for construction of **Trash Cans**.
