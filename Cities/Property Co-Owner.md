A **Property Co-Owner** is a type of [[Property Owner]] which co-owns a **Co-Owned Property**. A **Property Co-Owner** must obey all [[Property Owner]] rules and rights as well as **Property Co-Owner Rules** where rules or rights of the same name are overruled in favor of **Property Co-Owner Rules**.

---
## Property Co-Owner Rules
#### Signage
Co-Owned [[Property]] requires signage as if it were [[Property#Private Property|Private Property]], [[Property#Shared Private Property|Shared Private Property]] or [[Property#Publicized (Portions of) Private Property|Publicized Property]] respectively.
#### ⟬o⟭ Building Within Property Bounds
##### Co-Ownership Space
**Co-Ownership Space** is the [[Landarea]] of a **Co-Owned Property** which is fully mutable by the **Property Co-Owner**.
##### Easement Space
**Easement Space** is the [[Landarea]] of a **Co-Owned Property** which is mutable by the **Property Co-Owner** but subject to changes by the [[City Manager]].
##### Permittable Encroachment Space
**Permittable Encroachment Space** is the [[Landarea]] of a [[City]] and partially of **Co-Owned Property** which is only mutable with the approval of the [[City Manager]].
##### Encroachment Space
**Encroachment Space** is the [[Landarea]] of a [[City]] which would be considered a [[Crime]] to mutate. It is only mutable with the approval of the [[City Manager]].
##### Shared Space
**Shared Space** is the [[Landarea]] of a [[City]] which is shared between multiple neighboring **Property Co-Owners**. May require the approval of the [[City Manager]].
##### Outline Space
**Outline Space** includes both [[#Co-Ownership Space]] and [[#Easement Space]]. Outline space is where **Co-Owned Properties** must be outlined before leasing where the line falls on the edge (inside of) [[#Easement Space]] and [[#Permittable Encroachment Space]].
#### Separate Default Tax Amount for Co-Owned Property
Co-owned properties are charged subject to the [[City Manager]]'s rules. By default, a [[City Manager]] charges **Property Co-Owners** [[Land]] rent. Charges by the [[City Manager]] must follow [[Property Owner#Rent Limitations|Rent Limitations]].
#### ⟬o⟭ Edge Spacing
A **City Block** must follow the respective street encroachment zone rules as defined in [[#⟬o⟭ Building Within Property Bounds]].
#### Trash Cans
Constructions within a **City Block** must either contain a [[Trash Can]] or place a [[Trash Can]] on the street in front of the **City Block**.
#### City Provision Requirement for Buildings Outside of y80 to y120
Structures which height falls outside of a range between y80 and y120 must be approved by the [[City Manager]]. If a structure will be higher than y220 it must also be approved by an [[Elected Officials#Elected Official|Elected Official]].
#### Block Monopolization & Repossession
A single **Property Co-Owner** or group of **Property Co-Owner** working together as a party may not own more than 33% of [[City]] blocks in any given district. [[Land]] is subject having it's **Property Co-Owner** sub-divided by the [[Government]] &𝓇 [[City Manager]] if this rule is not met.

Additionally, **City Blocks** may not be bought up to force other users out of using **City Blocks**. If a **City Block** is not built on and not being used then it can rightfully be re-possessed by the [[City Manager]] in accordance with [[Property Owner#Demolition|Demolition]] jurisdiction.

Any [[Property]] within a 200 block square radius of the outermost **City Blocks** of a given zone may be requested to be [[Property Owner#Demolition|demolished]] by the [[Government]] on behalf of the request of a [[City Manager]] during an [[City Manager#Request Land Expansions|Land Expansion Request]]. However, [[Property Owner#Demolition|demolition]] cannot be forced.
#### Property Abandonment
A **Property Co-Owner** is not allowed to abandon a newly leased **Co-Owned Property** for 5 [[Taxes#Tax Period|Tax Periods]] after obtaining the property.

## Property Co-Owner Rights
#### ⟬o⟭ Right to Trade Land 
A **Property Co-Owner** is allowed to trade their [[Land]] block with the approval of the [[City Manager]].
#### Request City Resources
A **Property Co-Owner** is allowed to request resources from the [[City]] in order to assist in the operation or construction of their **Co-Owned Property**. The [[City]] is allowed to deny these requests assuming it does not violate [[Jurisdiction]] or [[City#Rules|City Rules]].
#### Property Accessibility
A [[City]] is required to provide an accessible path to any given **Co-Owned Property** within the [[City]]. A [[City]] may *not* trap [[Citizen|Citizens]] or make it difficult for [[Citizen|Citizens]] to enter or leave their **Co-Owned Property**. This includes creating [[Property]] restrictions which would cause [[Citizen|Citizens]] to only be able to proceed by accepting some type of [[Contracts|Contract]], [[Fines|Fine]] or other negative effect.

# Co-Owned Property
A **Co-Owned Property** is a block of land which is be co-owned by a [[Property Co-Owner]] or the [[Government]] alongside a [[City Manager]].
